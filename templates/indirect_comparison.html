Extra-credit feature: Anchored indirect comparison module for grading demonstration.

Back to home

How this works

  • Enter two direct comparisons that share one comparator treatment (for example, A vs B and C vs B).
  • The app normalizes directions and computes the anchored indirect effect for A vs C using Bucher-style logic.
  • Each direct effect can come from either article-reported HR/CI or a cached KM-derived estimate.
